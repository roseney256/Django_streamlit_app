About Methane Predictions App

This app uses machine learning models to predict methane emissions from agricultural operations based on input data such as weather, soil conditions, and livestock numbers.

The app is built with Django and Streamlit. Django is used for the web framework, while Streamlit provides the data visualization and machine learning components.

The machine learning models are trained on a large dataset of methane emissions from various agricultural operations. The models use various features such as temperature, precipitation, and livestock numbers to predict methane emissions with high accuracy.

The goal of this app is to help farmers and agricultural companies to better manage their methane emissions and reduce their impact on the environment.

For more information about the app, please contact us at info@sirge.com.
